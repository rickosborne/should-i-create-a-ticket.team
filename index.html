Should I create a ticket?
Yes, please.
But ... why?

Ticketing systems have one job: to help you manage all the things you need to think about for your project.

If you don't file a ticket, you're taking away the opportunity to let the team decide what to do with it.

Not everyone has a perfect memory — without a ticket, some people won't remember it.
